A journey shaped with our community

In under a decade we have grown from a passion project into a trusted education innovation partner.

2017 — Launch of EduSphere Labs

Piloted blended learning cohorts with 60 students focused on STEM fundamentals and design thinking.

2019 — Institutional partnerships

Secured collaborations with five universities to integrate EduSphere projects into their curriculum.

2021 — Launch of Mentor Collective

Introduced a network of vetted industry mentors delivering one-on-one coaching and portfolio feedback.

2024 — Global learning partners

Expanded into 35 countries and launched employer pathways with 40+ enterprise partners.
